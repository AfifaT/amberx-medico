About AmberX Medico

Established in 2007, AmberX Medico has been serving the healthcare needs of Aurangabad and surrounding communities with trust and dedication. We provide genuine medicines, surgicals, baby care, cosmetics, and FMCG products.

Our pharmacy is managed by S. T. Haque, a qualified B. Pharma graduate from Jamia Hamdard University, New Delhi, with over 15 years of experience as a Drug Expert & Consultant Pharmacist.

As a Registered Pharmacist (Reg. No. 21102/2007), he is committed to delivering the highest standards of professional care and ensuring the well-being of every customer.
